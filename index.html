Our Product

SEKAR is a natural hair mask formulated from canang waste, enriched with Impatiens balsamina (balsam flower) extract and Pandanus amaryllifolius (pandan) as the aroma, these ingredients are rich in flavonoids, tannins, saponins, and glycosides, which provide antioxidant, antibacterial, and strengthening effects to support healthier, more resilient hair.

Designed as a safe, eco-friendly, and affordable alternative to chemical based hair treatments, SEKAR helps reduce hair loss, improve moisture balance, and enhance overall hair condition. This innovative formulation not only promotes effective hair care but also supports the sustainable use of local resources by transforming ceremonial waste into a valuable and functional product.
